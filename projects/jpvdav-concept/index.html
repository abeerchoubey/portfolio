About Our School

Established in 1998 in the historic city of Katni, Madhya Pradesh

The JPV DAV Public School, Katni (MP), established in the year 1998, has been imparting education to the students of this historic city without any gender discrimination. Our school is situated in Pureni near F.C.I. Godown, providing a conducive learning environment for students.

We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also character, values, and life skills that prepare students for success in an ever-changing world.

Quality Education

Comprehensive curriculum designed to nurture young minds and build strong foundations.

Holistic Development

Focus on academic excellence alongside physical, emotional, and social growth.

Inclusive Environment

Education without gender discrimination, fostering equality and respect for all.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure with smart classrooms and advanced learning tools.

Principal's Message

An educational institution is just not about bricks, mortar and concrete but about building character, enriching minds and about enriching experiences that last a lifetime.

We recognize that parents are the first educators and we work in partnership with them to ensure the best possible outcomes for every child. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to fostering a love of learning and helping each student reach their full potential.

Together, we create an environment where students feel valued, supported, and inspired to excel in all aspects of their lives.
